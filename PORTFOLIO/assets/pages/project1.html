D & A Convenience Store

This project features a fully responsive, modern web interface crafted for a local convenience store, D & A. The design focuses on intuitive navigation, allowing customers to easily browse product categories, check store hours, and find contact details. The primary goal was to establish a polished, professional online presence that accurately reflects the store's offerings.
